template<typename T >
gsCrossApPatch ( const gsMultiPatch< T > &  boundary)

Constructs a spring patch object by a collection of tensor-product patches defining the boundaries of a patch.

The number of boundaries is expected to be 2*d, where d is the space dimension. They are expected to form a closed "shell"
