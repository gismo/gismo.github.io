template<class T>
class gismo::gsVisitorBiharmonic< T >

Visitor for the biharmonic equation.

Assembles the bilinear form

\[ (\Delta u,\Delta v)_\Omega \]

And the linear form

\[ (f,v)_\Omega. \]
